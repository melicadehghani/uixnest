UI/UX TOPICS

UX design and UI design are often used interchangeably, but actually, they describe very different things. UX design refers to the term “user experience design”, while UI stands for “user interface design”. While UI generally deals with the interaction between users and computer systems, UX deals more generally with a user's overall experience with a brand, product or service.
